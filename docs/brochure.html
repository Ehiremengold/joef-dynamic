The problem

Paper registers and hand-typed results cost time and credibility.

Most schools still run on manual marking and re-typed report sheets. That quietly costs hours every term — and the odd arithmetic slip on a result can dent a parent's trust in the school.

Hours lost every term marking scripts and re-typing scores into report cards by hand.

Avoidable mistakes on totals and positions that undermine the school's credibility.

Entrance exams that are hard to organise, mark, and rank fairly at scale.

Attendance kept in a register that never quite makes it onto the report card.

No single safe record of students, staff, and results — files get lost or damaged.

What your school can do

Everything below is live and working today.

The core platform is already built and running — not a promise. Each module is designed around how a secondary school actually operates.

Student roster

Live

Enrol students, auto-generate a secure sign-in PIN, search and filter by class, reset PINs, and deactivate leavers.

Staff & access control

Live

Administrator and teacher logins with clear roles. The system won't let the school lock itself out.

Multi-campus & arms

Live

Run several campuses from one account, with flexible arms per level (SS2A, SS2B…) — added and named freely.

Subjects & departments

Live

One subject list grouped into Science, Art, and Commercial, with per-student exemptions for subjects they don't take.

Computer-based testing

Live

Online tests with a server-side timer and auto-saved answers — a dropped connection never loses a student's work.

Common entrance exams

Live

Prospective pupils register and sit online, gated by a shared access code you switch on for exam day only.

Instant auto-marking

Live

Objective tests mark themselves accurately the moment they're submitted — no manual grading, no totting-up errors.

Branded result documents

Live

Letter-headed PDF result slips, full student transcripts, and ranked class result sheets — all carrying your logo and motto.

Term attendance

Live

Class teachers record days present against total school days per term — freely editable, and printed right on the result slip.

Sessions & terms

Live

Set up academic sessions and terms and flag the current one, so every result and register lands in the right period.

Secure cloud data

Live

Automatic backups, strict access rules, and separate protected logins for staff and students. Works on any device.

No installation

Live

Everything runs in the browser on phones, tablets, and computers — nothing for your staff to download or maintain.
